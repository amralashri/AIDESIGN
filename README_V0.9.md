# AIDESIGN v0.9 — Advanced Preliminary Concrete Checks

Added modules:

- beam shear and preliminary stirrup spacing;
- torsion demand flag;
- simplified development length;
- beam service deflection check;
- preliminary column axial-moment interaction ratio;
- minimum column longitudinal reinforcement;
- simplified slab punching-shear ratio;
- short- and estimated long-term slab deflection;
- top and bottom slab reinforcement labels;
- seismic detailing reminders;
- a permanent Advanced Concrete Checks dock.

These calculations remain preliminary. They are not a certified substitute for a complete SBC/ACI design and detailing review.
